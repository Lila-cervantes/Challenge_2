About Me

Born and raised in Chicago by immigrant parents. Family is a huge part of who I am and have been fortunate to travel to my familys native country, Mexico. I draw in my free time now but would love to dabble with a tattoo machine! I come from a very large family of 5 siblings and also being a twin. I love to volunteer and support my community. Web development has always sparked my interest but never knew where to start. I have a degree in Animation and Illustration. I've worked on a few murals around the city. I worked on screenprinting and sculpting. I love working with my hands and creating! Overall, welcome to my page! Look around.
